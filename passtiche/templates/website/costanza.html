Mobile Wallets, Made Simple.

The mobile wallet revolution has arrived.

Millions of businesses are now beginning to adopt mobile wallet technology.

Within a few years, mobile payments will have powered over a trillion dollars in transactions in both developing and emerging markets, and mobile wallets will be used for most of the new store cards and loyalty cards used by shoppers.

And the rate of mobile wallet adoption will increase as these wallets develop deep integration with mobile assistant apps like Apple Siri and Google Now.


Dozens of competing solutions

There is Apple Passbook, Google Wallet, and Windows Phone 8 Wallet. Square, Paypal, and dozens of smaller startups offering solutions. Mobile carriers are backing Isis. Credit card companies are backing V.me. And there are several international mobile wallet platforms like M-Pesa and Verafone SailPay.

This large and growing array of options is resulting in confusion for shoppers and burdensome support challenges for businesses and app developers.


A solution for simplicity

Costanza isn't a mobile wallet. It's a platform that simplifies mobile wallets, so that shoppers and businesses can focus on their transactions without getting hung up on which type of mobile wallet solution they need to use.

Passbook Badges for your Website

Our first product is called Passtiche. It's an online tool and SDK that makes it easy to instantly generate custom Passbook content with Add to Passbook badges that help customers preview and download the passes from any device.

Check out the documentation to get started.
